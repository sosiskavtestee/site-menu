Nicoise

The world-famous Nicoise salad comes from Nice. It would seem that lettuce leaves, tomatoes, olives, anchovies or juicy pieces of tuna seasoned with olive oil and supplemented with boiled eggs cut into quarters-what could be easier? However, in the right combination of these available ingredients - the whole secret of the popularity of the salad "Nicoise".
