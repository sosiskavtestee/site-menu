The English Queen's favorite salad

It is known that Elizabeth II is extremely unpretentious in food. But monarchs also have their weaknesses. Her Majesty, for example, is crazy about bitter arugula. In this favorite recipe of the queen, arugula is successfully combined with blue cheese. In the original, the main English cheese, stilton, is involved in the role of retinue, of course, but even with its more affordable analogues, the salad looks like a blue-blooded dish.
