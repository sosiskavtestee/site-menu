Pavlova's Dessert

The cake is named after the great Russian ballerina Anna Pavlova during her tour of Australia and New Zealand in 1926. At that time, it was fashionable to name various items and dishes in her honor. Pavlova's dessert is a meringue cake with fresh fruit, most correctly (imho) with strawberries. They are baked in the form of a cake or roll, which is cut in portions, or a cake.
